Grouptechno is a small company. We are not a fat clumsy cat, but rather like a Cheetah. Our employees are always a little hungry, so we try to develop software as quickly as possible. Like any technology company, we are committed to exponential growth, so we are happy to take on a multi-year project with generous funding. We are not going to tell you now about Agile, frameworks and that without Scrum our employees are not capable of intimacy, because it's already being shouted at every corner. We'll just take your money and implement your projects with AI, BigData, Cloud Services, Neuronet, VR and other technologies. We have no scalability issues for any projects.
